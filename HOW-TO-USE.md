# How to Use the Barefoot Academy System

**A simple guide for coaches, office staff, accountants, managers, and parents.**

You do not need to know computers deeply. If you can use email and Facebook, you can use this system.

---

## Part 1 — Opening the system

### Step 1: Open the website

1. Open **Chrome**, **Edge**, or **Safari** on your phone or computer.
2. Type the website address your academy gave you (the same link you use for the public site).
3. Press Enter.

### Step 2: Log in

1. Click **Login**.
2. Type your **email**.
3. Type your **password**.
4. Click the button to sign in.

### Step 3: Where you land after login

| You are… | You will see… |
|----------|----------------|
| Coach or branch office staff | **Home page** for your branch |
| Main office (all branches) | **Head office** page with every branch |
| Parent or guardian | **Family page** — only your child’s information |

**Forgot your password?** Ask your branch manager. They can set a new one for you.

---

## Part 2 — What each type of user can do

### Head office manager (main academy office)

- See **all branches** at once
- Compare branches (fees collected, students, attendance)
- Add new branch locations
- Create logins for staff
- Post notices on the website

### Branch manager

- Everything for **your branch only**
- Add students and confirm them as **full members**
- Approve who is officially registered
- Add many students from a list (spreadsheet)
- See history of who changed student or fee records

### Accountant or cashier

- Create **fee bills** and record **payments**
- Track who still owes money
- Send reminders (including WhatsApp)
- Manage **uniform stock** and sales reports
- Record **branch expenses** (rent, travel, etc.)
- Print reports for the office

### Coach or general staff

- Add and edit **student details**
- Mark **attendance** every class
- Record **belt promotions** and print certificates
- **Cannot** see fee screens (unless your manager gives you accountant access)

### Parent or guardian

- See your **child’s belt, branch, and coach**
- See **attendance** for this month
- See **fee bills** — paid and still owed
- Read **notices** from the academy
- **Cannot** edit information or pay online here

## Part 4 — Students (the most important part)

### Words you will see

| Word in the system | Meaning |
|--------------------|---------|
| **Pending** | New student — not fully registered yet. Temporary member number. |
| **Official** / full member | Registered with head office. Can attend, get ID card, and be billed. |
| **PRE-** number | Temporary ID while pending |
| **BFN-** number (or similar) | Official member ID after confirmation |

### Add one new student

1. Click **Students** on the left.
2. Click the button to **add** a new student.
3. Fill in:
   - Name  
   - Phone  
   - Branch (location)  
   - Date they joined  
   - Belt colour  
   - Parent name and phone  
   - Photo (if you have one)  
4. Click **Save**.

They are **Pending** until a manager confirms them.

### Confirm a student as a full member (official)

**Only branch manager or head office can do this.**

1. Open the student’s name.
2. Check that **name, branch, join date, and belt** are filled in.
3. Click **Mark official registration**.
4. The member number changes to the official type.

**After this you can:** mark attendance, print ID card, and create fee bills.

### Tabs when you open a student

At the top of the student page you will see tabs:

| Tab | What is there |
|-----|----------------|
| **Profile** | Photo, phone, buttons (ID card, WhatsApp, parent login) |
| **Attendance** | How often they came this month |
| **Fees** | Their bills and payments (office staff) |
| **Uniforms** | Uniform items bought through the office |
| **Certificates** | Belt certificates — can open and print |

### Add many students from a list (managers)

1. Click **Import students**.
2. Download the **sample file**.
3. Open it in **Excel**, fill one row per student, save.
4. Upload the file **or** type names on the screen.
5. Open each student and **confirm as full member** when they actually join.

### Give a parent their own login

1. Open the student → **Profile** tab.
2. Click **Create parent login**.
3. Enter parent’s name, email, and a password (tell them in person or by phone).
4. Parent goes to the website → **Login** → uses that email.

### Print an ID card

1. Student must be a **full member (official)**.
2. Open student → **Profile**.
3. Click **Download ID card**.
4. Open the file and **print**.  
   The card has a small **square code** — scanning it helps with check-in.

## Part 5 — Attendance (every class day)

### Mark one day — student by student

1. Click **Attendance** on the left.
2. Choose the **date** (usually today).
3. For each name, choose:
   - **Present** — came on time  
   - **Late** — came but late  
   - **Absent** — did not come  
4. It saves when you pick an option.

### Mark the whole class at once

1. Click **Bulk attendance**.
2. Choose the date.
3. Mark Present / Late / Absent for each row.
4. Click **Save all**.

### Warning: students who stopped coming

The screen may show names of students who have **not come for about two weeks**. Call the family to check on them.

### Check-in with the square code on the ID card

- **Family scanning the code** — can see belt and member status (information only).
- **Coach who is logged in** — scanning can mark the student **Present** for today.

---

## Part 6 — Belts and certificates (after grading)

1. Click **Belt promotions** (or open a student and click the belt button).
2. Choose the student.
3. Enter the **new belt colour** and **date** of the exam.
4. Save.

If it says the student is **not ready yet**, they may need to wait longer since the last exam (your office decides how many months).

To **print a certificate**: open the student → **Certificates** tab → open the certificate file → print.

---

## Part 7 — Fees and money (office staff)

> Parents **do not pay on the website**. They pay at the branch (cash, bank, etc.). You record payment in the system.

### Create a fee bill

1. Click **Invoices** (this means **bills**).
2. Click to **create** a new bill.
3. Choose a **full member** student.
4. Tick what you are charging: admission, monthly fee, uniform, belt, gloves, etc.
5. Add uniform from stock if you are selling one.
6. Set **due date** (when payment is expected).
7. If the student has a **scholarship** (free), use the scholarship option so the total is zero.
8. Save.

If they paid immediately, you can enter the payment on the same screen.

### When they pay at the counter

1. Open the bill.
2. Type how much they paid and how they paid (cash, bank, card).
3. Save.
4. You can print a **receipt** (proof of payment).

If they paid everything, you can use **Pay balance in full** instead.

### Three papers you can print

| Paper | When to use |
|-------|-------------|
| **Invoice** (full bill) | Full details for office records |
| **Payment slip** | Hand to family — “please pay this amount” |
| **Receipt** | Hand to family — “we received your payment” |

### See who still owes money

1. Click **Fee tracking** — list of unpaid bills.
2. Click **Fee reminders** — opens **WhatsApp** with a ready message for each family.

Main office can also send **automatic morning emails** if the family email is saved in the system.

## Part 8 — Uniforms and stock

1. Click **Inventory**.
2. See how many items you have (sizes, types).
3. **Adjust stock** when new uniforms arrive or something is damaged.
4. **Transfer** when you send items to another branch.

When you sell on a **fee bill**, the count goes down by itself.

**Uniform sales report:** click **Uniform sales** to see what was sold this month. You can download a file and open it in Excel.

---

## Part 9 — Branches, class times, events

### Branch address and phone

Click **Branches** → click your branch name.

### Class timetable

Click **Schedules** → **Add class** → fill branch, class name, day (Monday…), time, coach name.

### Tournaments and events

1. Click **Events** → **New event**.
2. Write title, date, fee, description.
3. Turn on **show on website** if families should see it online.
4. Open the event and **register** each student who is joining.

---

## Part 10 — People who registered on the website

When someone fills **Register online** on the public site:

1. Click **Online signups** in the menu.
2. You see their name and phone.
3. Click **Convert** to start a student file (still pending).
4. When they actually join the branch, complete details and **confirm as full member**.

---

## Part 11 — Branch expenses

Click **Expenses** → **Add expense**.

Write date, type (rent, travel, equipment…), amount, and a short note.  
Use the month filter to review one month.

## Part 12 — Reports (managers and accountants)

| What you need | Where to click |
|---------------|----------------|
| How your branch did this month | **Branch reports** — can print |
| File for head office | **Summary reports** — save or print |
| Is our branch keeping records complete? | **Compliance** — shows scores |

## Part 13 — Sending messages to families

Click **Notifications**.

1. Choose the student.
2. Choose **Email** to send (if email is set up at your academy).
3. Write your message and send.

For unpaid fees, use **Send reminder** on the bill screen, or **Fee reminders** for WhatsApp.

---

## Part 14 — For parents (families)

### How you get a login

The branch office creates it when your child joins. You get an **email** and **password**.

### Each time you visit

1. Go to the academy website → **Login**.
2. If you have more than one child, pick the child at the top of the page.
3. You can view:
   - Name, belt, branch, coach  
   - **Attendance** this month (percentage)  
   - **Bills** — what is paid and what is still due  
   - **Notices** from the academy  

### Important for parents

- You **cannot** change your child’s details here — call the branch.
- You **cannot** pay online here — pay at the branch as you normally do.
- The office updates payments when you pay.

## Part 18 — When something goes wrong

| Problem | What to do |
|---------|------------|
| Cannot mark attendance | Student may still be **Pending**. Manager must **confirm as full member** first. |
| I do not see fee / bill menus | You may be logged in as **Coach only**. Ask manager for accountant access. |
| Parent cannot log in | Check **Create parent login** was done and email is correct. |
| “Not eligible” for belt | Student needs more time since last exam — check with head office rule. |
| Payment slip vs receipt? | Slip = please pay. Receipt = thank you, we received money. |
| Website not opening | Check internet; ask the person who manages the academy website server. |
| Anything else | Call your **branch manager** or **head office**. |

## Part 19 — Daily checklist (branch office)

Print this and tick each day:

- [ ] Check **Online signups** — call new enquiries

- [ ] Send fee reminders if families are late
